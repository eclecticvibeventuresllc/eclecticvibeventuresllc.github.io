Privacy Policy for Lofi Loop Haven

Last updated: May 3, 2025

Lofi Loop Haven is a minimalist ambient sound layering app created by Eclectic Vibe Ventures LLC. We care about your peace of mind — not just your chill vibes — so here’s how we handle your data:

🔒 What We Collect

We don’t collect personal data. There’s no login, sign-up, or user account required.

📊 Anonymous Analytics

We may receive basic anonymous stats from Google Play (like install counts or crash data) to improve stability. These are managed by the Google Play Console and are not tied to your identity.

🎚️ App Preferences

Your sound layer settings, volume levels, and saved combination are are stored locally on your device. We do not see or send this data anywhere.

📦 Third-Party Tools

Lofi Loop Haven does not use advertising networks, in-app trackers, or third-party SDKs beyond the Play Console itself.

🔁 Changes

We may update this policy if our app features change. The latest version will always be available at this URL.
