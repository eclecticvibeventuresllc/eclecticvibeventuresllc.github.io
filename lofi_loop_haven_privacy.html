Privacy Policy for Lofi Loop Haven

Last updated: May 18, 2025

Lofi Loop Haven is a minimalist ambient sound layering app created by Eclectic Vibe Ventures LLC. We care about your peace of mind — not just your chill vibes — so here’s how we handle your data:

🔒 What We Collect

We don’t collect personal data. There’s no login, sign-up, or user account required.

📊 Anonymous Analytics

Google Play may provide basic aggregated statistics (like install counts) and anonymous crash reports to help us improve stability. These reports contain device information (e.g., Android version) but no personally identifiable data.

🎚️ App Preferences

Your sound layer settings, volume levels, and saved combination are stored locally on your device. We do not see or send this data anywhere.

📦 Third-Party Tools

Lofi Loop Haven uses only essential Android libraries provided by Google and the Android Open Source Project. These components do not collect personal data for our purposes. We do not use advertising networks, analytics SDKs, or third-party trackers.

💳 In-App Purchases

Payment processing for add-ons is handled entirely by Google Play. We do not store or have access to your payment information.

🔐 Data Security

Since all your preferences and audio combinations are stored locally on your device, we recommend using device security features to protect your data.

🔁 Changes

We may update this policy if our app features change. The latest version will always be available at this URL.

📬 Contact

If you have questions, ideas, or concerns, please reach out via email: lofiloophavenprivacy+eclecticvibeventures@gmail.com
