Privacy Policy for Emotion Sanctuary

Last updated: April 13, 2026

Emotion Sanctuary is a minimalist sound layering and healing frequency app created by Eclectic Vibe Ventures LLC. We care about your peace of mind — not just your calm — so here’s how we handle your data:

What We Collect

We don’t collect personal data. There’s no login, sign-up, or user account required.

Data Stored on Your Device

Your app preferences are stored locally on your device only, including:

  • Sound layer selections, volume levels, and saved custom presets
  • Session tools settings (timer, end tone)
  • Language override preference (if you change it in Settings)

In-App Purchases & Trials

Payment processing for add-ons is handled entirely by Google Play. We do not store or have access to your payment information. Trial status is tracked locally on your device and can be reset by clearing app data.

Downloadable Sound Packs

Some sound packs are delivered on-demand by Google Play for offline use. These audio files are stored on your device. You can remove them by clearing the app’s storage or uninstalling the app.

🗑️ Data Deletion

Emotion Sanctuary stores preferences locally on your device. We do not store personal data on our servers.

To delete all local data:

  • Android: Uninstall the app or use Settings → Apps → Emotion Sanctuary → Storage → Clear Data

If you would like to make a formal data deletion request or have questions about data handling, contact us at emotionsanctuaryprivacy+eclecticvibeventures@gmail.com

Anonymous Analytics & Crash Reports

We do not use third-party analytics SDKs. However:

  • Google Play provides aggregated install stats and anonymized crash/ANR reports (for example: Android version, device model).

These cannot identify you and are not stored by us.

Third-Party Tools

Emotion Sanctuary uses essential platform libraries provided by Google for Android (including Google Play Billing and Play Asset Delivery). These components do not collect personal data for our purposes. We do not use advertising networks, marketing trackers, or non-essential third-party SDKs.

Data Security

Your preferences stay local to your device. For privacy:

  • Use your device's security features (PIN, fingerprint, Face ID, passcode)
  • Uninstall/Delete the app to wipe all data instantly

Changes

We may update this policy if our app features change. The latest version will always be available at this URL.
